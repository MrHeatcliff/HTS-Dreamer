# Loss Scale Audit V4

Current status: structural reduction audit complete; real per-loss same-batch gradient norms were generated by `dreamerv3.hts_debug_trace` and exported to `per_loss_gradient_norms_v4.json`.

The deterministic one-batch trace confirms the earlier imbalance concern: raw `L_temp` and `L_vc` are much larger than `L_hier` and `L_sdyn`, and the `L_temp` gradient is concentrated in the projector, head 1, and trunk. No paper-default weights were changed in this package.

| Loss | Reduction | Effective Denominator |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `L_hier` | mean over feature, batch, time, then weighted mean over levels | B*T*feat_dim per decoder plus level averaging |
| `L_sdyn` | mean over feature, masked mean over valid batch-time, weighted mean over levels | valid_windows*feat_dim per level plus level weights |
| `L_temp` | masked InfoNCE mean over valid positive pairs | valid_positive_pairs |
| `L_vc` | projection flattened over batch*time; mean variance and mean off-diagonal covariance | projection_dim and projection_dim*(projection_dim-1) |
| `L_sparse` | mean abs over batch, time, features, then mean over levels | B*T*head_dim*levels |
